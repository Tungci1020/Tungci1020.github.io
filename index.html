I'm a third-year Physics undergraduate, currently working on non-Hermitian physics under Prof. Zhesen Yang. My research focuses on non-Hermitian physics, with additional interests in quantum field theory and general relativity.

Beyond physics, I'm an avid motorsport enthusiast—particularly GT3, Hypercar, and Formula 1—with a keen interest in aerodynamics. My favourite driver is Max Verstappen, and I support Scuderia Ferrari and Toyota Racing. I also enjoy literature, music, and cinema; my favourite writers are Balzac and Proust.
